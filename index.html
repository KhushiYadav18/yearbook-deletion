Yearbook App - Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go! If you'd like to delete your account from the Yearbook App, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Yearbook App on your device.
  2. Go to the settings section of the app.
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account".

Please note: Account deletion is irreversible. All data associated with your account will be permanently removed.

Data Deletion Policy:

Once your account is deleted, all personal data, including photos and account information, will be permanently erased from our servers. If you have any concerns about data privacy, feel free to contact us at web@sarc-iitb.org.
